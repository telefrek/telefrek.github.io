## Who We Are

Telefrek, Inc. is a software development company based in Sanford, NC. This policy covers our website at telefrek.com.

## What We Collect

This is a static website hosted on GitHub Pages. We do not run a backend server, database, or user authentication system.

**What we do not collect:**
- We do not collect personal information through forms or accounts
- We do not use cookies for tracking or advertising
- We do not sell or share any data with third parties

**What may be collected automatically:**
- GitHub Pages may collect basic server logs (IP addresses, browser type, pages visited) as part of standard web hosting. This is governed by [GitHub's Privacy Statement](https://docs.github.com/en/site-policy/privacy-policies/github-general-privacy-statement).
- If we enable analytics in the future, we will update this policy before doing so.
